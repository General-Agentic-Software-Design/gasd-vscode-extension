THE VALIDATE AMOUNT > 0.5 AS TYPE.CASH QUESTION
================================================

Question: Does this work?

Syntax-wise: ✓ YES
Semantically: ⚠ UNCLEAR
Recommended: ✗ NO

Why unclear:
    • Does the Cash TYPE binding apply to the whole expression?
    • Does it apply just to the amount parameter?
    • If amount > 0.5 is false, does validation still happen?

Better approach (both HYBRID and MANDATORY):
    1. VALIDATE amount AS TYPE.Cash              // Validate type
    2. ENSURE amount > 0.5 OTHERWISE THROW ...   // Check condition

This clearly separates:
    • Step 1: Type contract validation
    • Step 2: Business logic validation
